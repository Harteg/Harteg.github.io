CrookCatcher

Have you got irrespective friends, a curious little brother or are you simply afraid that your phone will get stolen? - Then CrookCatcher is for you! CrookCatcher guards your phone and tablet as a secret photographer, ready to snap a photo of anyone who unsuccessfully continuously tries to unlock your device. CrookCatcher does not just take a photo, but it also finds out where it is, using GPS and network capabilities. CrookCatcher then notifies you of the event by sending you the photo and location to your email, so you always know if you have an intruder.

Features

  • Take photo with the front facing camera on incorrect unlock attempt
  • Custom number of failed unlock attempts before CrookCatcher takes action
  • Receive email notification with photo and location
  • Uses the default Android Lock Screen
  • CrookCatcher only starts when it needs to, so it has no significant impact on your battery
  • Browse photos with location on the device
